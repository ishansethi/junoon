Story

Our Culinary team have developed a exemplary menu that combines Junoon’s signature dishes – mouthwatering entrée, range of hearty mixed platters, authentic curries and side dishes to compliment all of them. And expensive selection of wines from all parts of the world are available to choose from our menu.
